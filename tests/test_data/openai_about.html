About OpenAI

OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company. We're dedicated to creating safe artificial general intelligence (AGI) that benefits all of humanity.

Founded in 2015 by Sam Altman, Elon Musk, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, Wojciech Zaremba, and John Schulman, OpenAI has grown to become one of the world's leading AI research organizations.

Our team of over 300 researchers and engineers work on cutting-edge AI systems, from large language models like GPT to advanced robotics and safety research.

Our Mission

We're committed to ensuring that artificial general intelligence—AI systems that are generally smarter than humans—benefits all of humanity. We're building safe, beneficial AGI, but we're also committed to sharing our knowledge and helping others do the same.
